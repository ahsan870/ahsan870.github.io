About me

I'm Ahsan Ahmed, a Software Engineer with over 7 years of experience in designing and building enterprise-grade applications.

My core expertise lies in Java Spring Boot development and supporting large-scale applications in the telecommunications industry. Over the years, I've also been deeply involved in infrastructure design, CI/CD pipeline automation, and cloud engineering across AWS, Docker, Kubernetes (EKS, TKGS), and traditional virtualization platforms.

Alongside my professional work, I completed a Bachelor's in Computer Information Systems and I'm currently pursuing an MS in Analytics at Georgia Tech.

I'm passionate about the intersection of software engineering and data science—bridging reliable systems with intelligent decision-making. My interests include Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Neural Networks, and applied MLOps. I thrive at the edge where new technologies meet production-grade reliability, and I'm always curious to explore what's next in IT and AI.
